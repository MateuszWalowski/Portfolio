About me

My name is Mateusz Wałowski, I am 23 years old and I have moved from Poland to study Multimedia Design at KEA. I like trying out new challenges and learning new things. I’d describe myself as a shy, although very cooperative person. I’m very interested in the Scandinavian culture, which is one of the reasons why I have moved here. Other than that I am keen about many types of visual arts and music, especially ones that deal with more serious and darker topics.
