Basic web

Responsive website

The goal of the first theme was to teach us basic knowledge about user interface design and responsive web design. In this assigment we were supposed to design a responsive website with at least 2 breakpoints using media queries and css grid structure. My website has 2 breakpoints, at 600 pixels and 900 pixels of viewport width.

I have used many grids throught my website, since they are an easy way to implement responsivness to the website. I have also decided to use responsive size units, such as em, vh, vw and %. Breakpoints on the website affect structure of the grids, white space and the navigation bar.

Design sprint

In this assigment we were designing an app for KEA students, following the Google design sprint method. Our group has chosen to design an app that would help students to find events and interest groups in our school. We had 5 days to design it, and every day was a different phase of the sprint.

We have learnt various methods of sketching, such as four-step sketch and Crazy 8s. We also had to learn how to discuss the problem in larger group, for example using sprint questions and HMW (How Might We?) notes. Every decision was being discussed in the group and we all worked on optimal solutions for our prototype.

Onboarding

In this assigment we had to create an onboarding website that uses gamification elements and explains an important aspect about moving to Denmark. My group decided that our onboarding app would help user to learn about the most important documents he might need after moving to the country.

I have coded and designed the website myself, with other members helping me with illustrations and text. We achieved gamification of the site by having our user progress through the screens, and going through the quiz at the end. Every time user answers a question there is a sound indicating correct or wrong answer. After passing the quiz user is rewarded with congratulations screen.
